Prayer Time In Dhaka,Bangladesh

Dhaka Prayer Time is important to know daily by the residents of Dhaka to perform daily their prayers (صلوٰۃ - نماز). Daily Dhaka Prayer Times include Fajar Times at 5:11 AM, Dhuhur / Zuhur Prayer Time at 12:12 PM, Asr Prayer Time at 4:20 PM, Maghrib Prayer Time at 5:58 PM and Isha Prayer Time at 7:13 PM in Dhaka.
